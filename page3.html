Hiking/ Trekking Tips

Trekking in Nepal can range from easy walks to high-altitude adventures. Always carry enough water, acclimatize properly, and hire a guide for safety.

Food and water Saftey

Drink bottled or filtered water only. Street food can be delicious but make sure it is freshly cooked. Popular dishes include momo and dal bhat etc.

Best Time to Visit

The best months to visit are March–May (spring) and September–November (autumn). Clear skies, pleasant temperatures, and vibrant festivals make these seasons ideal.
